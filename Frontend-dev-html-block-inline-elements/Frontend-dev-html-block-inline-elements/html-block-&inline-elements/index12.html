Words of Wisdom

On Dreams

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action." – Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

On Potential

"Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached." – Swami Vivekananda

On Action

"An ounce of practice is worth more than tons of preaching." – Mahatma Gandhi

Quotes inspire people because they are concise, powerful distillations of wisdom and experience. They have the ability to offer a fresh perspective, provide a burst of motivation, or remind us of fundamental truths in just a few words. By connecting us to the thoughts of great minds, they make us feel less alone in our struggles and more confident in our aspirations, serving as timeless beacons of guidance and encouragement.
